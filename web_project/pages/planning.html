Target Audience

The target audience of my website is for all past, present and future missionaries for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. It also includes missionary families and those interested in the church or learning more about Jesus Christ. Their wants and needs are to learn more about missionary work, tips and tricks in order to be a more effective missionary, and read personal accounts from a missionary currently serving with the hardships and blessings that come from it.
